About the newsletter

We will send carefully curated content from around the web about web and application development. These will include articles, tutorials, blog posts, and tweets!

We will also be keeping a pulse on the local community and any events, hackathons, job openings, or cool things happening in the Jackson & Mississippi technology communities.

We welcome everyone to contribute to the newsletter by emailing us at mailinglist@developers.ms with any content you think the community will enjoy, we will even give you a shoutout!
